About Us

Serving with compassion, integrity, and purpose to help communities thrive with dignity and opportunity.

Rooted in service, focused on people

Who we are

Zarrah Foundation is a registered nonprofit society in Bangladesh (Registration S-15000/2026), registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. Founded in 2026, we are a non-political, humanitarian, community service organization committed to uplifting disadvantaged communities.

Why we exist

We exist because too many individuals and families still face barriers to education, health awareness, safety, livelihoods, and social support. Zarrah Foundation works to bridge those gaps with practical, community-centered action that strengthens dignity, resilience, and inclusion.

What we do

Our programs empower disadvantaged communities through education, healthcare awareness, humanitarian assistance, skills development, social welfare, and sustainable community initiatives. We aim to respond to immediate needs while also supporting long-term growth and self-reliance.
